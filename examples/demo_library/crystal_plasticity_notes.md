# Crystal Plasticity Demo Notes

This local note describes a crystal plasticity literature review workflow.

The first theme is CPFEM validation for BCC ferritic steel. Important evidence
includes EBSD texture evolution, macroscopic stress-strain curves, and model
parameters such as initial slip resistance and hardening rate.

The second theme is transferability. A model calibrated for one strain rate or
heat treatment may not transfer to another condition without recalibration.
